## Introduction

Selecting the right motor is crucial for any mechatronics system. This guide covers the essential parameters you need to consider.

## Key Parameters

1. **Torque (T):** The rotational force required to move the load.
2. **Speed (ω):** The required angular velocity.
3. **Power (P):** Calculated as P = T × ω.
4. **Voltage and Current:** Ensure your power supply and motor driver can handle the motor's requirements.

## Step-by-Step Selection

1. Determine the load requirements.
2. Calculate the required torque, including a safety factor.
3. Determine the required speed.
4. Choose a motor technology (DC, Stepper, Brushless).
5. Verify thermal constraints.
