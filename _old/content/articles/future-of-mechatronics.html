It's about deep integration where software defines the behavior of mechanical hardware. ### Key Components 1. **Sensing**: Capturing real-world data with precision. 2. **Processing**: High-speed control loops using RTOS or embedded Linux. 3. **Actuation**: Precise motion control with high torque density. [!NOTE] Modern mechatronic systems often leverage Digital Twins for predictive maintenance.
